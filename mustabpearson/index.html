ARE YOU PATRIOTIC ?

Patriotism is the love and respect citizens show for their country through actions, values, and culture. In Ghana, it connects deeply with our traditions, unity, and shared identity.

PATRIOTISM THROUGH
CULTURE & TRADITION

Patriotism in Ghana is more than a word - it is a living expression of our culture and traditions. It is found in the rhythms of our drums, the colours of our kente, the stories passed down through generations, and the values that shape how we live as a people.

It is the farmer who rises at dawn, continuing a legacy of hard work rooted in our heritage. The teacher who nurtures young minds, passing on knowledge and values that define who we are. The young innovator building solutions inspired by Ghanaian identity. The market woman whose resilience reflects the strength and unity of our communities.

To be patriotic in Ghana is to live our culture daily - not only in celebration, but in responsibility.

Our culture teaches us who we are.
Our traditions guide how we live.
And through them, we build a stronger Ghana.

TOGETHER
WE ARE
GHANA

The "Sankofa Ghana" campaign is a call to every citizen, at home and abroad, to celebrate what makes us uniquely Ghanaian. From our vibrant culture to our shared values, from our rich history to our bright future, this campaign is about rekindling the fire of patriotism in every heart.

Commissioned by Ministry of Information, this initiative especially targets the youth of Ghana, empowering the next generation to lead with pride, unity, and purpose.

CAMPAIGN GOALS

Using culture and tradition to inspire pride, unity, and active citizenship in Ghana.

Promote Cultural Pride

Encourage Ghanaians to take pride in their traditions, clothing, food, and cultural identity.

Celebrate Traditions

Highlight festivals, music, dance, and customs as a way of preserving Ghanaian heritage.

Strengthen Unity Through Culture

Use shared traditions and values to bring people together across regions and communities.

Preserve Cultural Knowledge

Educate younger generations about Ghanaian history, symbols, and traditional values.

Connect the Diaspora

Strengthen the bond between Ghanaians at home and abroad through shared cultural identity.

Engage the Youth

Encourage young people to express and promote culture through creativity and digital platforms.

A CALL TO
OUR CULTURE

Ghana’s greatest strength has always been its culture the traditions, values, and identity that shape who we are as a people. From our festivals and music to our food and clothing, culture is the foundation of our unity and pride.
We call on the youth the storytellers, the creators, and the future leaders to embrace and carry forward our cultural heritage. Let it guide your actions, inspire your creativity, and connect you to your roots.
Together, through our shared traditions and values, we can build a stronger, more united Ghana. Our culture is not just our past it is our future.

EXPLORE GHANA

Ghana is home to over 100 ethnic groups, each with their own language, customs, and traditions. Yet through this diversity runs a common thread of warmth, hospitality, and pride. From the colourful kente of the Ashanti to the smock-weaving of the north, from the drumming of the Ga to the dances of the Ewe — every tradition adds a vibrant thread to the tapestry of what it means to be Ghanaian. From our traditions and festivals to our food and
fashion, culture defines who we are as a people.
